Andrew Luv & The Franchise Players
Genre-Bending Soul · Pop · Smooth Jazz · Hip Hop · R&B

Andrew Luv & The Franchise Players bring a dynamic blend of Soul, Pop, Smooth Jazz, Hip Hop, and R&B to the stage. This five-piece band delivers the energy of a big band with the finesse of seasoned pros.

From festivals and corporate galas to weddings and private events, their polished performances, versatile setlists, and engaging stage presence make them one of Central Florida’s premier show groups.
