Our Story

Andrew Luv And The Franchise Players
Genre-Bending Soul / Pop / Smooth Jazz / Hip Hop / R&B

Andrew Luv And The Franchise Players deliver a vibrant fusion of Soul, Pop, Smooth Jazz, Hip Hop, and R&B—creating a genre-defying sound that’s as timeless as it is fresh. Led by the charismatic and seasoned front man Andrew Luv, whose vocal mastery has captivated audiences for nearly four decades, this five-piece ensemble brings unmatched energy, musicality, and sophistication to every stage.

From festivals and upscale dinner venues to intimate weddings and private events, Andrew Luv’s commanding presence and emotive delivery set the tone for an unforgettable live experience.


The Players
Each member of The Franchise Players is a journeyman musician with deep roots in performance and production, contributing to the band’s rich, full-bodied sound:

- Vince Belvin – Music Director, Keys, Sax, Vocals
- Vernal Archie – Guitar, Vocals
- Barney Bonaparte – Bass, Vocals
- Elliott Glenn – Drums, Vocals

Together, this dynamic quintet delivers the sonic impact of a full big band, blending tight arrangements with spontaneous flair. Their chemistry on stage is entertaining and pulls you in. Each performance is a musical journey that transcends genre and expectation.
